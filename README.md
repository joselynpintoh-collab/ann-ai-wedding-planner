# ANN AI Wedding Planner

## Overview

ANN is an AI-powered wedding planning product designed to help brides manage the emotional and practical complexity of planning a wedding. It brings together inspiration, budgeting, timelines, reminders, and decision support in one elegant experience.

This concept was created with a clear problem in mind: many brides do not have a wedding planner, are managing everything on their own, and need a smarter, more supportive system to stay organized without losing the joy of the process.

## The Problem

Wedding planning can quickly become overwhelming. Brides often have to manage multiple decisions, vendors, deadlines, budgets, family expectations, and creative ideas all at once.

Most existing tools feel either too transactional, too generic, or visually uninspiring. They help with task tracking, but they do not truly feel like a thoughtful assistant.

ANN was designed to fill that gap.

## The Solution

ANN is an AI wedding planning assistant that helps brides:

- organize their vision and inspiration
- keep track of budget categories and spending
- manage timelines, deadlines, and pending decisions
- get personalized support through an AI assistant
- collaborate with the people helping them plan
- reduce stress while keeping the process beautiful and intentional

## Who It Is For

ANN is designed for:

- brides planning their own wedding
- brides without a full-service wedding planner
- busy women balancing work, school, family, and wedding planning
- users who want both beauty and practicality in one product
- brides who need guidance, structure, and emotional support during the process

## Core Features

### 1. Ask ANN
A conversational AI assistant that helps users navigate wedding planning questions, make decisions, and generate ideas based on text prompts and uploaded inspiration.

Examples:
- suggest decor ideas based on my venue
- help me build a romantic garden wedding theme
- what should I prioritize this month
- generate ideas from the reference photos I uploaded

### 2. Index
A central navigation space that gives the bride a clean and editorial-style overview of the product and makes each section easy to access.

### 3. Vision
A creative space where users can upload inspiration, save references, and use ANN to generate ideas aligned with their wedding aesthetic.

### 4. Budget
A budgeting section that helps users track estimated vs actual spending, organize categories, and understand where the money is going.

### 5. Timeline
A practical planning section focused on:
- important dates this month
- tasks to do
- pending decisions
- deadlines
- collaboration with others helping in the process

### 6. Setup
An onboarding flow where the bride can define:
- name
- wedding date
- location
- wedding structure
- theme
- support team helping plan the wedding

## Product Vision

The vision for ANN is to create a wedding planning experience that feels:

- elegant
- editorial
- emotionally supportive
- highly useful
- AI-native

Rather than feeling like a checklist tool, ANN is meant to feel like a smart and stylish planning companion.

## Design Direction

The product design is inspired by a high-fashion editorial aesthetic.

Key visual principles:
- minimal and refined interface
- warm paper-like background tones
- strong serif typography
- large titles and elegant spacing
- image-led inspiration
- clean structure without heavy UI clutter

The goal is to make wedding planning feel beautiful, calm, and elevated.
